About Us

ESP32 Project Library is a free educational resource built for students, hobbyists, and engineers who want practical ESP32 tutorials in one place. Our goal is to make IoT and embedded automation easier to learn with clear wiring guides, working code, and real-world project ideas.

Whether you are building your first sensor circuit or prototyping a connected product, you will find step-by-step tutorials across agriculture, home automation, security, robotics, AI, and more.

Every project page includes parts lists, schematics, source code, applications, and FAQs — so you can move from idea to working prototype faster.
